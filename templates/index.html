Pose Detection

We've developed a sophisticated system using camera technology to detect hand and face movements in real-time.This innovation transforms user-device interactions, offering a seamless and intuitive experience, from gesture-based commands to secure authentication. Embracing the power of computer vision, our project envisions a future where technology responds effortlessly to human presence and gestures, redefining the way we engage with our devices.
